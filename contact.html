FAQ

Common Questions

Do you take advance reservations?

Yes! You can reserve a table via WhatsApp or by calling us directly. We recommend booking in advance for weekends and groups of 6 or more.

Is parking available?

Yes, ample free parking is available right in front of the restaurant along NH-16. We can accommodate cars, bikes and small trucks.

Do you offer takeaway or parcel orders?

Absolutely. Just WhatsApp us or call +91 98765 43210 to place your parcel order. We pack everything neatly and it will be ready for pickup.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and all major debit/credit cards. No minimum order for card payments.

Are children welcome?

Of course! We're a family restaurant at heart. High chairs are available and our menu has plenty of mild dishes kids love.

Do you cater for events or large orders?

Yes, we offer bulk catering for events, parties and functions. Contact us on WhatsApp with your requirements and date for a custom quote.
